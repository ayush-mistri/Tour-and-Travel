ITINERARY

  • DAY 1

    • Arrive at Tirupati Airport or Tirupati Railway Station.
    • Check-in at your hotel in Tirupati.
    • Freshen up and rest.
    • In the evening, visit the local market for shopping and try some South Indian cuisine at a local restaurant.
    • Overnight stay in Tirupati.

  • DAY 2

    • Wake up early and start your journey to Tirumala, where the Tirupati Balaji Temple is located. You can take a taxi or use the frequent bus services.
    • Upon reaching Tirumala, check into your accommodation if you have a reservation.
    • Visit the Tirupati Balaji Temple and experience darshan (viewing of the deity). Be prepared for potential long queues, especially during peak times.
    • Explore other attractions in Tirumala, such as the Sri Padmavathi Ammavari Temple and Akasa Ganga waterfall.
    • In the evening, attend the evening aarti at the Tirupati Balaji Temple if you wish.
    • Return to your accommodation in Tirumala or Tirupati.

  • DAY 3

    • Visit Chandragiri Fort, a historical fort known for its architecture and history.
    • Have lunch in Chandragiri or en route to Srikalahasti.
    • In the afternoon, visit the Srikalahasti Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva.
    • Explore the town and its surroundings, and don't miss the opportunity to see the imposing Raja Gopuram (temple tower).
    • Return to Tirupati for the night.

  • DAY 4

    • Check out from your hotel in Tirupati.
    • Visit the Sri Varasiddhi Vinayaka Temple in Kanipakam, known for its unique idol.
    • Have lunch in Kanipakam or en route to your departure point.
    • Depending on your departure time, you may have some free time to explore more of Tirupati or do some last-minute shopping.
    • Depart from Tirupati Airport or Tirupati Railway Station, concluding your trip.
