ITINERARY

  • DAY 1

      MORNING

    • Arrive at Diu Airport or Diu Bus Station.
    • Check-in at your chosen hotel or guesthouse.
    • Freshen up and have a quick rest.

      • AFTERNOON

    • Head to Naida Caves, a unique geological formation, for some exploration.
    • Enjoy lunch at a local restaurant serving fresh seafood.
    • Visit the Gangeshwar Temple, known for its five shiva lingas located amidst the rocks.

      • EVENING

    • Spend your evening at Diu Fort. Explore the fort's architecture and enjoy the panoramic views from the lighthouse.
    • Return to your accommodation for the night.

  • DAY 2

      MORNING
    • Have breakfast at your hotel.
    • Head to Ghogla Beach for a relaxing morning by the sea.

      • AFTERNOON
    • Lunch at a beachfront shack or a local restaurant.
    • Visit Nagoa Beach for water sports activities such as parasailing, jet skiing, and banana boat rides.

      • EVENING
    • Stroll along Jallandhar Beach and admire the impressive statue of Lord Hanuman.
    • Enjoy a beachfront dinner at one of the seafood restaurants.

  • DAY 3

      MORNING
    • Visit the St. Paul's Church, an exquisite example of baroque architecture.
    • Explore Diu Museum, which houses artifacts and exhibits showcasing the history of Diu.
    • Have lunch at a local eatery.

      • AFTERNOON
    • Spend some leisure time at Chakratirth Beach, known for its peaceful atmosphere.
    • Explore the local markets and shop for souvenirs.

      • EVENING
    • Visit the INS Khukri Memorial, dedicated to the naval officers who lost their lives during the 1971 Indo-Pak war.
    • Enjoy a farewell dinner at a restaurant of your choice.

  • DAY 4

      MORNING
    • Have breakfast and check out from your accommodation.
    • Depart from Diu with beautiful memories and souvenirs.
