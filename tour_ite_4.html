ITINERARY

  • DAY 1

    • Arrive at Diu Airport or Veraval Railway Station (the nearest major transportation hubs).
    • Check-in at your hotel in Somnath.
    • Visit Somnath Temple, one of the 12 Jyotirlinga shrines of Lord Shiva. Witness the evening aarti ceremony at the temple, which is a mesmerizing experience.
    • Dinner at a local restaurant.
    • Overnight stay in Somnath.

  • DAY 2

    • After breakfast, explore the surroundings of Somnath. Visit places like Bhalka Tirth, Triveni Sangam, and Panch Pandava Gufa.
    • Enjoy lunch at a local restaurant.
    • Spend some leisure time at Somnath Beach.
    • In the evening, you can visit the Somnath Museum to learn more about the history of the temple.
    • Dinner at your hotel or a local restaurant.
    • Overnight stay in Somnath.

  • DAY 3

    • Check out from your hotel in Somnath after breakfast.
    • Drive to Dwarka (approximately 4-5 hours by road).
    • Check-in at your hotel in Dwarka.
    • Visit Dwarkadhish Temple, one of the Char Dham temples in India.
    • Explore the nearby Nageshwar Jyotirlinga Temple and Gomti Ghat.
    • Dinner at a local restaurant.
    • Overnight stay in Dwarka.

  • DAY 4

    • After breakfast, visit Bet Dwarka by taking a boat ride from the Okha Jetty. Explore the Dwarkadhish Temple on the island and other sites.
    • Return to Dwarka for lunch.
    • Visit Rukmini Devi Temple, Gopi Talav, and the beautiful Dwarka Beach.
    • In the evening, attend the evening aarti at Dwarkadhish Temple, a truly divine experience.
    • Dinner at your hotel or a local restaurant.
    • Overnight stay in Dwarka.

  • DAY 5

    • After breakfast, check out from your hotel.
    • Depart from Dwarka for your onward journey from Diu Airport or Veraval Railway Station.
