• 7 days tour
  • Up to 20 people
  • 4 tour guides
  • Sleep in private tents
  • Difficulty: medium
  • price: $100

ITINERARY

  • DAY 1

      MORNING

    • Arrive at Goa's Dabolim Airport or the railway station.
    • Check-in to your hotel or accommodation.
    • Freshen up and relax for a while.

      • AFTERNOON

    • Head to one of Goa's beautiful beaches, like Baga Beach or Calangute Beach.
    • Enjoy the sun, sand, and sea.
    • Have lunch at a beachside shack or a nearby restaurant.

      • EVENING

    • Explore the vibrant markets like Anjuna Flea Market or Mapusa Market.
    • Shop for souvenirs, clothing, and local handicrafts.
    • Dinner at a beachfront restaurant with live music.
