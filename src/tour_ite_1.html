ITINERARY

  • DAY 1

      MORNING

    • Arrive at Goa's Dabolim Airport or the railway station.
    • Check-in to your hotel or accommodation.
    • Freshen up and relax for a while.

      • AFTERNOON

    • Head to one of Goa's beautiful beaches, like Baga Beach or Calangute Beach.
    • Enjoy the sun, sand, and sea.
    • Have lunch at a beachside shack or a nearby restaurant.

      • EVENING

    • Explore the vibrant markets like Anjuna Flea Market or Mapusa Market.
    • Shop for souvenirs, clothing, and local handicrafts.
    • Dinner at a beachfront restaurant with live music.

  • DAY 2

      MORNING
    • After breakfast, visit Palolem Beach, known for its serene beauty.
    • You can opt for a morning yoga session on the beach if available.

      • AFTERNOON
    • Try water sports activities such as parasailing, jet-skiing, or banana boat rides.
    • Have lunch at a beachside café or shack

      • EVENING
    • Explore the nearby Butterfly Beach or Agonda Beach for a quieter atmosphere.Watch the sunset at any of the beaches.
    • Dinner at a beachside restaurant or at a popular seafood joint.

  • DAY 3

      MORNING
    • Visit Old Goa, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and explore historic churches like Basilica of Bom Jesus and Se Cathedral.
    • Learn about the Portuguese influence on Goan architecture and culture.

      • AFTERNOON
    • Enjoy a traditional Goan lunch at a local restaurant.
    • Visit the Museum of Christian Art and St. Augustine Tower for more historical insights.

      • EVENING
    • Take a leisurely stroll through Fontainhas, the Latin Quarter of Panaji, known for its colorful houses.
    • Dinner at a Goan restaurant to savor local delicacies like vindaloo and seafood dishes.

  • DAY 4

      MORNING
    • Check out from your hotel.
    • Drive to Dudhsagar Waterfalls for a thrilling jeep safari or trek to the falls.
    • Have a picnic lunch near the falls or at a local eatery.

      • AFTERNOON
    • Shop for souvenirs, clothing, and local handicrafts.
    • Head to the airport or railway station for your onward journey.
