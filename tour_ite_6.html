ITINERARY

  • DAY 1

    • Arrive at Haridwar or Rishikesh, which are common starting points for the journey to Kedarnath.
    • Check-in at your hotel in Haridwar or Rishikesh.
    • Attend the evening Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri in Haridwar or at Parmarth Niketan in Rishikesh.
    • Overnight stay in Haridwar or Rishikesh.

  • DAY 2

    • Depart from Haridwar or Rishikesh for Guptkashi, a town located en route to Kedarnath.
    • The journey to Guptkashi can take around 8-10 hours by road.
    • Check-in at your hotel or guesthouse in Guptkashi.
    • Rest and prepare for the upcoming trek to Kedarnath.
    • Overnight stay in Guptkashi.

  • DAY 3

    • Early morning, start your journey to Gaurikund, which is the base camp for the Kedarnath trek.
    • From Gaurikund, trek to Kedarnath. The trek is approximately 14 kilometers and can take around 6-8 hours, depending on your pace.
    • Reach Kedarnath and check-in at your accommodation.
    • Visit the Kedarnath Temple and attend the evening aarti.
    • Overnight stay in Kedarnath.

  • DAY 4

    • Early morning, visit the Kedarnath Temple for darshan (viewing of the deity).
    • After darshan, start the trek back to Gaurikund.
    • From Gaurikund, return to Guptkashi.
    • Check-in at your hotel or guesthouse in Guptkashi.
    • Rest and relax after the trek.
    • Overnight stay in Guptkashi.

  • DAY 5

    • Depart from Guptkashi and head back to Haridwar or Rishikesh.
    • The journey can take around 8-10 hours.
    • Check-in at your hotel in Haridwar or Rishikesh.
    • Overnight stay in Haridwar or Rishikesh.

  • DAY 6

    • Depending on your departure schedule, you can explore more of Haridwar or Rishikesh.
    • Depart from Haridwar or Rishikesh for your onward journey.
