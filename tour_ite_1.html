ITINERARY

  • DAY 1

    • Arrive at Bhuntar Airport (Kullu-Manali Airport) or Joginder Nagar Railway Station. Both are well-connected to Manali.
    • Drive to Manali (approximately 1.5 to 2 hours).
    • Check-in at your chosen accommodation.
    • Rest and acclimatize to the altitude.
    • In the evening, take a stroll on the Mall Road and explore local markets.
    • Dinner at a local restaurant.
    • Overnight stay in Manali.

  • DAY 2

    • Breakfast at your hotel.
    • Visit the Hadimba Devi Temple, a historic wooden temple surrounded by lush greenery.
    • Explore Manu Temple, dedicated to the sage Manu, and enjoy the serene surroundings.
    • Visit the Vashisht Hot Springs, where you can take a dip in the natural hot water springs.
    • Afternoon: Lunch at a local restaurant.
    • Explore Old Manali, known for its charming cafes, shops, and vibrant atmosphere.
    • Visit Tibetan Monastery to witness Tibetan culture and shop for handicrafts.
    • Dinner at a restaurant in Old Manali.
    • Overnight stay in Manali.

  • DAY 3

    • Breakfast at your hotel.
    • Drive to Solang Valley (around 30-40 minutes), famous for adventure activities.
    • Engage in activities like paragliding, ziplining, ATV rides, and ropeway rides.
    • Enjoy breathtaking views of snow-capped mountains.
    • Lunch at Solang Valley.
    • Return to Manali in the afternoon.
    • Visit the Museum of Himachal Culture & Folk Art to learn about local traditions.
    • Dinner at a local restaurant.
    • Overnight stay in Manali.

  • DAY 4

    • Early morning departure (around 4 AM) for Rohtang Pass (subject to weather and road conditions).
    • Enjoy scenic views, play in the snow, and indulge in snow activities.
    • Visit Rahala Falls and Marhi on the way.
    • Return to Manali in the late afternoon.
    • Lunch on the way back or at your hotel.
    • Relax or explore Manali's markets in the evening.
    • Dinner at your hotel or a local restaurant.
    • Overnight stay in Manali.

  • DAY 5

    • Breakfast at your hotel.
    • Visit the Naggar Castle, a historic castle with beautiful architecture and views.
    • Explore Nicholas Roerich Art Gallery nearby.
    • Have lunch in Naggar or Manali, depending on your preference.
    • Spend some time shopping for souvenirs in Manali.
    • Check out from your hotel.
    • Depart for Bhuntar Airport or Joginder Nagar Railway Station for your onward journey.
