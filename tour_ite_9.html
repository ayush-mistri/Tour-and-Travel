ITINERARY

  • DAY 1

      MORNING

    • Arrive at Veer Savarkar International Airport in Port Blair.
    • Meet your local guide or transfer to your hotel.
    • Check in and freshen up at your chosen hotel or resort.

      • AFTERNOON

    • Visit the Cellular Jail, a historic site associated with India's freedom struggle.
    • Attend the captivating light and sound show at Cellular Jail in the evening, which provides insights into the island's history.

  • DAY 2

      MORNING
    • Take a ferry to Ross Island, known for its historical ruins and lush greenery.
    • Explore the remnants of British colonial architecture and enjoy the local wildlife.

      • AFTERNOON
    • Head to North Bay Island for snorkeling and water sports activities.
    • Return to Port Blair in the evening.

  • DAY 3

      MORNING
    • Check out from your Port Blair accommodation.
    • Transfer to the Port Blair Harbour for a ferry ride to Havelock Island (approximately 2.5-3 hours).
    • Check in at your Havelock Island hotel or resort.

      • AFTERNOON
    • Relax on the renowned Radhanagar Beach, often ranked as one of the world's best beaches.
    • Enjoy the clear blue waters and pristine sands.

  • DAY 4

      MORNING
    • Embark on a snorkeling trip to Elephant Beach, known for its vibrant coral reefs and marine life. You can also enjoy other water activities like glass-bottom boat rides or sea walking.

      • AFTERNOON
    • Return to your hotel for lunch.
    • Spend your afternoon at leisure, relaxing on the beach or exploring nearby areas.

  • DAY 5

      MORNING
    • Visit Kalapathar Beach, known for its stunning sunrise views, white sands, and serene ambiance.
    • Capture some memorable photos and enjoy the tranquil atmosphere.

      • AFTERNOON
    • Explore the local markets, indulge in some shopping, and try out local cuisine.
    • Visit the nearby beaches or opt for spa treatments if desired.

  • DAY 6

      MORNING
    • Check out from your Havelock Island accommodation.
    • Take a ferry to Neil Island (approximately 1-1.5 hours).
    • Check in at your Neil Island hotel or resort.

      • AFTERNOON
    • Explore Neil Island's natural beauty by visiting Laxmanpur Beach, Natural Bridge, and Bharatpur Beach.
    • Enjoy the serene atmosphere and take in the scenic views.

  • DAY 7

      MORNING
    • Check out from your Neil Island hotel.
    • Take a ferry back to Port Blair.
    • Visit the Chidiya Tapu, known for its lush greenery and stunning sunset views.

      • AFTERNOON
    • Depending on your departure time, you may have some free time to explore Port Blair or do some last-minute shopping for souvenirs.
    • Transfer to Veer Savarkar International Airport for your onward journey.
